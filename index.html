About Me

I am a first year graduate student in the Oregon State University Robotics program. My research interests are in path planning and learning for autonomous robots.

Current Work

I currently am working on autonomous underwater navigation, and tether managment for underwater robots. I work in the Robot Decision Making Lab at Oregon State University, with Dr. Geoff Hollinger.

Previous Work

I completed my B.S. in Computer Science with an emphasis in AI and Machine Learning at Northwestern Universiy. There, I developed an interest in robotics. As an undergraduate, I worked with Dr. Brenna Argall in the ARGALLAB at Northwestern University, working on planning and localization on a smart wheelchair.
